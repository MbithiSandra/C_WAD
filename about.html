About Us

History

The farm began as a 100 acre farm inherited by the owner from the father whereby the land has been an ancentral land from beginning of time.The first animal was brought into the farm in the year 2003 and from then the farm has grown drastically

Information about the farm

The farm currently accomodates over 400 animals of different kinds which include about not limited to sheep,goats,chicken,cows and even bees. The ranch has employed over 50 workers whereby 80% come from the neighbouring villages. There is also farm tours and trainings which occur to help other farmers achieve the same level of success. We also sell the different products we acquire from the animals in our ranch. We also have a section of the ranch where we grow crops and sell to the community at favourable prices

Mission statement

At Kyase Ranch, our mission is to cultivate a sustainable and harmonious relationship between people, animals, and the land. We are committed to ethical farming practices,integrating technology and communtiy engagement
